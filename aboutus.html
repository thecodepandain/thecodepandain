Vision And Mission

Our Mission

Learning a new language isn't a piece of cake and we understand that.
Being stuck in a phase where the concept is well known but the use of it is not can be problematic. Thus, Code Panda brings you a platform that provides a number of practice problems and assignments to make learning a choice, not a necessity.

Our Vision

We are not perfect and we know that. Therefore making a stage where enthusiastic learners like you can be more creative is our vision. Making it playful and not a burden is what pushes us to move forward in this journey.

Why Are We Using Characters

For us, Characters are the main feature of this platform. We don't want to make coding boring for our students or they think that
they have to be serious and professional to learn to program. We use characters as rewards. We have Stories
based questions, we have tons of MCQ assignments. A student will solve them step by step and
Earn rewards unlock their character, buys weapons. These rewards can be redeemed
as goodies and can be used as trophies to your friends.
